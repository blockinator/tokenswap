Blocktorial MIGRATION

We’ve made the migration process for Blocktorial as simple as possible.

What you need to prepare:

  • Ready access to the Private Key of your MetaMask or other Ethereum-supported browser wallet.
  • Tronlink (most popular TRON wallets) Chrome extension installed.

IMPORTANT: DO NOT send Blocktorial directly from your cold wallet (Trezor/Ledger/etc.) or exchange account! YOU WILL LOSE IT! If your Blocktorial is stored in one of these, please send/withdraw the Blocktorial to your MetaMask wallet first!

STEP 1

Log in to your MetaMask - make sure you have access to the private key for this wallet.

Once you are logged in, your current Blocktorial balance will be shown on the screen below. Fill out the amount you wish to swap, and hit the “SEND” button to complete this step.

You will need to wait a few minutes for confirmation due to transaction times on the Ethereum network.

Please make sure you have enough funds in your MetaMask account to cover the gas fees.
